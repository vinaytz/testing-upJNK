A job description is a written document that outlines the responsibilities, qualifications, and skills required for a job. It's usually created by the employee in charge of recruiting, with help from the company's human resources department. A job description typically includes the following sections: Job title: A brief description of the job role Job purpose: A high-level overview of the role's scope and responsibilities Job duties and responsibilities: A description of the tasks and duties assigned to the role Required qualifications: A list of the skills, education, experience, and other knowledge required to perform the job Preferred qualifications: Additional qualifications that can help determine if a candidate will be productive and successful in the role Working conditions: Information about the working conditions for the role A job description can help with: Attracting the right candidate A clear job description can help attract qualified candidates Boosting engagement and retention
